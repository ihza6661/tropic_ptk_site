## ‚≠ê Getting Reviews (Critical for Local SEO)

### **Why Reviews Matter:**
- Reviews are #1 local ranking factor
- 10+ reviews = better Local Pack chances
- 4.5+ star avg = trust & conversions
- Recent reviews = freshness signal

### **How to Get Reviews:**

**1. Create Direct Review Link:**

Format: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_PLACE_ID]/review`

To find your place ID:
1. Go to your GBP listing

## üö® Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ‚ùå **Keyword stuffing business name**
   - Wrong: "Tropic Coffee Shop Pontianak 24 Jam WiFi"
   - Right: "Tropic di Merdeka"

2. ‚ùå **Inconsistent info across platforms**
   - GBP, website, social media must match
   - Address, hours, phone must be identical

3. ‚ùå **Ignoring reviews**
   - Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48h
   - Engaged businesses rank better

4. ‚ùå **Using stock photos**
   - Only use real photos of YOUR location
   - Google can detect & penalize stock images

5. ‚ùå **Not posting regularly**
   - Dead profiles = lower rankings
   - Post 1-2x per week minimum

6. ‚ùå **Creating duplicate listings**
   - One location = one profile
   - Duplicates will be suspended

7. ‚ùå **Wrong category**
   - Primary MUST be "Coffee Shop"
   - Don't use generic "Restaurant"
